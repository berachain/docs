Proof of liquidity (PoL) rewards liquidity contributions through validator-allocated Reward Vault emissions. As the native dApp powering trading liquidity in the Berachain ecosystem, many BEX pools are eligible for PoL rewards.

The process for earning PoL rewards through BEX involves these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide liquidity">Deposit liquidity into your chosen BEX pool.</Step>
  <Step title="Stake LP tokens in a reward vault">
    After providing liquidity, you receive LP tokens. Stake these tokens in the pool's staking
    contract.
  </Step>
</Steps>

View and manage your staked positions in reward vaults on [Bera Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/vaults) (Vaults section).

## Whitelisting pools for PoL rewards

If you are a project that uses BEX for your tokens' liquidity, you can apply to have your pool(s) whitelisted for Reward Vault emissions. This process runs through [Berachain governance](/general/governance/reward-vault-governance).
