---

Follow these steps to deposit into and withdraw from a Bend vault.

## Requirements

- Wallet with $HONEY
- Native $BERA for transaction fees

<Tip>
If you don't have $HONEY, get it on [Berachain Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/swap) or [HoneySwap](https://honey.berachain.com).
</Tip>

## Depositing

Deposit $HONEY to earn native lending yield and PoL $BGT yield.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bend and connect wallet">
    Go to [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/lend) and connect your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-connect-wallet.png" alt="Bend deposit connect wallet" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a vault">
    Choose a vault to deposit $HONEY into.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-choose-vault.png" alt="Bend choose vault" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure deposit">
    1. Select **Supply**
    2. Enter the $HONEY amount to deposit
    3. Choose **Stake** or **Supply & Stake** (recommended for PoL $BGT yield)
    4. Click **Review** to open the confirmation modal

    <Tip>
    You can enable **Supply & Stake** later if you skip it now.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-configure-deposit.png" alt="Bend configure deposit" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve, supply, and stake">
    Your wallet will prompt you to:

    1. Approve the vault to spend your $HONEY
    2. Supply $HONEY to the vault
    3. Receive receipt tokens
    4. Stake the receipt tokens in a Berachain PoL Reward Vault for $BGT yield

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-supply-stake.png" alt="Bend supply and stake" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-successful-supply-stake.png" alt="Bend successful supply and stake" />
    </Frame>

    You should see your deposited value (supplied and staked) and the receipt token amount.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-staked-receipt-tokens.png" alt="Bend staked receipt tokens" />
    </Frame>

    With your deposit staked, you can claim $BGT yield.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/deposit-yields.png" alt="Bend deposit yields" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawing

Unstake and withdraw your $HONEY.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure withdrawal">
    In the vault you want to withdraw from:

    1. Select **Withdraw**
    2. Enter the $HONEY amount to withdraw

    <Tip>
    If your balance shows 0, your deposit is staked. Unstake first, then withdraw.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-configure-withdraw.png" alt="Bend configure withdrawal" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unstake deposit">
    Where your staked balance is shown, click **Unstake**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-unstake.png" alt="Bend unstake deposit" />
    </Frame>

    Enter the amount to unstake and confirm.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-confirm-unstake.png" alt="Bend confirm unstake" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-successfully-unstaked.png" alt="Bend successfully unstaked" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw deposit">
    After unstaking, your balance appears under **Deposited**. Withdraw from there.

    1. Enter the $HONEY amount to withdraw
    2. Select **Withdraw**

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-specify-amount.png" alt="Bend specify withdrawal amount" />
    </Frame>

    Confirm the withdrawal in your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-confirm-withdrawal.png" alt="Bend confirm withdrawal" />
    </Frame>

    When complete, your $HONEY is back in your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/withdraw-successful-complete.png" alt="Bend successful withdrawal" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
