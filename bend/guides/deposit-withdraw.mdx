This guide will walk you through the process of contributing to a Bend Vault.

## Requirements

- Wallet with $HONEY
- Native $BERA to process transactions

<Tip>
If you do not have $HONEY, you can use [Berachain Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/swap) or [HoneySwap](https://honey.berachain.com).
</Tip>

## Depositing into Bend vaults

This will walk you through the steps of interacting with Bend and depositing $HONEY to gain native lending yields and additionally PoL $BGT yields.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bend and connect wallet">
    Your first step is to go to [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/lend) and connect your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-connect-wallet.png" alt="Bend deposit connect wallet" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a vault">
    Choose a Vault that you would like to deposit your $HONEY with.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-choose-vault.png" alt="Bend choose vault" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure deposit">
    1. Make sure to select **Supply**
    2. Specify a $HONEY amount you'd like to deposit
    3. Choose if you'd like to **Stake** or **Supply & Stake** (recommended for PoL $BGT yields)
    4. Click **Review** to bring up a modal to confirm

    <Tip>
    If you do not enable **Supply & Stake** now, you can still do so later.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-configure-deposit.png" alt="Bend configure deposit" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve, supply, and stake">
    Your wallet prompts you to:

    1. Approve transfer of the $HONEY token for the Vault
    2. Supply the $HONEY to the Vault
    3. Receive receipt token
    4. Stake receipt to a Berachain PoL Reward Vault for additional $BGT yields

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-supply-stake.png" alt="Bend supply and stake" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-successful-supply-stake.png" alt="Bend successful supply and stake" />
    </Frame>

    You should now see the successfully deposited total value, both supplied and staked, along with the corresponding amount in receipt tokens.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-staked-receipt-tokens.png" alt="Bend staked receipt tokens" />
    </Frame>

    With your current deposit staked, this now makes your account eligible to claim $BGT yields.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-deposit-yields.png" alt="Bend deposit yields" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawing from Bend vaults

This will walk you through the steps of withdrawing your deposited and staked tokens with Bend.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure withdrawal">
    While in the Vault you'd like to withdraw from:

    1. Make sure to select **Withdraw**
    2. Specify a $HONEY amount you'd like to withdraw

    <Tip>
    You will notice that your balance is showing 0, that is because your current deposit is staked and will require it being unstaked before withdrawing.
    </Tip>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-configure-withdraw.png" alt="Bend configure withdrawal" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unstake deposit">
    Where your current balance of stake is shown click the **Unstake** button.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-unstake.png" alt="Bend unstake deposit" />
    </Frame>

    You will be prompted to specify the amount you want to unstake and confirm.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-confirm-unstake.png" alt="Bend confirm unstake" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-successfully-unstaked.png" alt="Bend successfully unstaked" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw deposit">
    Now with your deposit unstaked, it is now shown in the **Deposited** section which allows you to withdraw your deposit.

    1. Specify a $HONEY amount you'd like to withdraw
    2. Make sure to select **Withdraw**

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-specify-amount.png" alt="Bend specify withdrawal amount" />
    </Frame>

    You'll be prompted to confirm the withdrawal with your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-confirm-withdrawal.png" alt="Bend confirm withdrawal" />
    </Frame>

    Once complete, you will have successfully withdrawn your deposit.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-withdraw-successful-complete.png" alt="Bend successful withdrawal" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
