---
title: "Borrow & Repay"
description: "Guide to borrowing and repaying on Bend"
---

This guide will walk you through the process of providing collateral to borrow $HONEY, and repaying the loan to avoid liquidation.

## Requirements

- Wallet with collateral (WETH, WBERA, WBTC, etc) listed on [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow)
- Native $BERA to process transactions

<Tip>
If you do not have any of the collateral listed, you can use [Berachain Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/swap).
</Tip>

## Supplying collateral and borrowing

This will walk you through the steps of interacting with Bend, supplying $WBERA as collateral to take out a loan in $HONEY.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bend and connect wallet">
    Your first step is to go to [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow) and connect your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-connect-wallet.png" alt="Bend borrow connect wallet" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a market">
    Choose a market that you can offer as collateral.

    This guide uses $WBERA.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-choose-market.png" alt="Bend choose market" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure borrow">
    1. Make sure to select **Borrow**
    2. Specify a $WBERA amount you'd like to deposit
    3. Click **Review** to bring up a modal to confirm

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-configure-borrow.png" alt="Bend configure borrow" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and supply">
    1. Approve transfer of the $WBERA token to the market
    2. Supply the $WBERA to the market as collateral

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-approve-supply.png" alt="Bend approve and supply" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-successful-approve-supply.png" alt="Bend successful approve and supply" />
    </Frame>

    You should now see the successfully supplied collateral and total value in the market.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-successful-supply.png" alt="Bend successful supply" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Borrow">
    1. Specify a $HONEY amount to borrow
    2. Ensure that the borrow is below the LTV limit to avoid liquidation
    3. Click **Review** to bring up a modal to confirm

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-borrow.png" alt="Bend borrow" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and borrow">
    Approve the borrow of $HONEY.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-approve-borrow.png" alt="Bend approve borrow" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-successful-approve-borrow.png" alt="Bend successful approve borrow" />
    </Frame>

    You should now see the successfully borrowed $HONEY and its value reflected in the market.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-successful-borrow.png" alt="Bend successful borrow" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Avoid liquidation">
    Monitor your position carefully to ensure it is not liquidated if your LTV exceeds the Liquidation Loan-To-Value (LLTV).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-borrow-monitor-ltv-lltv.png" alt="Bend monitor LTV and LLTV" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Repay and withdraw

You can now repay your loan with $HONEY and withdraw your collateral in $WBERA.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure repay">
    1. Select the **Repay** section
    2. Specify the amount of $HONEY you wish to repay
    3. Specify the amount of $WBERA collateral you wish to withdraw
    4. Click **Review** to open a confirmation modal

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-repay-configure-repay.png" alt="Bend configure repay" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm repay">
    Confirm the repayment transaction.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-repay-confirm-repay.png" alt="Bend confirm repay" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-repay-successful-confirm-repay.png" alt="Bend successful confirm repay" />
    </Frame>

    You should now see the successful repayment showing a zero balance.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend-repay-successful-repay.png" alt="Bend successful repay" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully borrowed and repaid a $HONEY loan on Bend.
