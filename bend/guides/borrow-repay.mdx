---
title: "Borrow & Repay"
description: "Supply collateral, borrow HONEY, monitor LTV/LLTV, and repay to withdraw collateral on Bend."
---

Follow these steps to supply collateral, borrow $HONEY, and repay to free your collateral.

## Requirements

- Wallet with collateral (WETH, WBERA, WBTC, etc.) listed on [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow)
- Native $BERA for transaction fees

<Tip>If you don't have collateral, swap on [Berachain Hub](https://hub.berachain.com/swap).</Tip>

## Supply collateral and borrow

Supply $WBERA as collateral and borrow $HONEY.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bend and connect wallet">
    Go to [Bend](https://bend.berachain.com/borrow) and connect your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-connect-wallet.png" alt="Bend borrow connect wallet" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a market">
    Choose a market where you can supply collateral. This guide uses $WBERA.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-choose-market.png" alt="Bend choose market" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure borrow">
    1. Select **Borrow**
    2. Enter the $WBERA amount to supply as collateral
    3. Click **Review** to open the confirmation modal

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-configure-borrow.png" alt="Bend configure borrow" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and supply">
    1. Approve the market to spend your $WBERA
    2. Supply $WBERA as collateral

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-approve-supply.png" alt="Bend approve and supply" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-successful-approve-supply.png" alt="Bend successful approve and supply" />
    </Frame>

    You should see your supplied collateral and total value in the market.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-successful-supply.png" alt="Bend successful supply" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Borrow">
    1. Enter the $HONEY amount to borrow
    2. Keep borrow below the LTV limit to avoid liquidation
    3. Click **Review** to confirm

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-borrow.png" alt="Bend borrow" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and borrow">
    Approve the borrow in your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-approve-borrow.png" alt="Bend approve borrow" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-successful-approve-borrow.png" alt="Bend successful approve borrow" />
    </Frame>

    You should see your borrowed $HONEY and its value in the market.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-successful-borrow.png" alt="Bend successful borrow" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Avoid liquidation">
    Monitor your position. If your LTV exceeds the Liquidation LTV (LLTV), your position can be liquidated.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/borrow-monitor-ltv-lltv.png" alt="Bend monitor LTV and LLTV" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Repay and withdraw

Repay $HONEY and withdraw your $WBERA collateral.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure repay">
    1. Select **Repay**
    2. Enter the $HONEY amount to repay
    3. Enter the $WBERA amount to withdraw
    4. Click **Review** to open the confirmation modal

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/repay-configure-repay.png" alt="Bend configure repay" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm repay">
    Confirm the repayment in your wallet.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/repay-confirm-repay.png" alt="Bend confirm repay" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/repay-successful-confirm-repay.png" alt="Bend successful confirm repay" />
    </Frame>

    You should see a zero balance after the repayment.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/bend/repay-successful-repay.png" alt="Bend successful repay" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now supplied collateral, borrowed $HONEY, and repaid to withdraw your collateral on Bend.
